## Quick Start Guide

### Step 1: Create Your First Note
1. Click the **"+ New Note"** button in the sidebar
2. Start typing your study material
3. The AI will automatically generate a title and categorize it

### Step 2: Generate a Quiz
1. Open any note
2. Click **"Generate Quiz"** in the AI Assistant panel
3. Take the quiz and review your results

### Step 3: Ask Questions
1. Type your question in the AI chat
2. Get instant answers based on your notes
3. Build on the conversation for deeper understanding

### Step 4: Track Your Progress
1. Visit the **Dashboard** to see your study analytics
2. Review your subject distribution
3. Set goals and track completion
